der - masculine
er, en, el, ig, ich, ismus, or , us
Days, months, seasons → der Montag (Monday), der Sommer (summer)
Weather elements → der Regen (rain), der Wind (wind)
Male people & animals → der Vater (father), der Löwe (lion)
Most alcohols → der Wein (wine), der Whisky (whisky) (except "das Bier")

die - feminine
e, heit, keit, ung, ion, ik, ie, enz/anz, ur
Female people & animals → die Mutter (mother), die Katze (cat)
Most German rivers → die Donau (Danube), die Elbe (Elbe)
Names of airplanes, motorcycles, and ships → die Boeing 747, die Titanic

das - nutral
chen, lein, ment, um, tum, 
Young people & baby animals → das Baby (baby), das Kätzchen (kitten)
Most nouns starting with "Ge-" → das Gebäude (building), das Geschäft (business)
Metals & chemical elements → das Gold (gold), das Eisen (iron)
Infinitives used as nouns → das Essen (eating/food), das Leben (life)

---------------
das - face gesicht, single ear ohr, single hair haar, chin kinn
der - head kopf, mouth mund, tooth zahn. 
die - rest plural ohr(en), haren, auge(n), nase, lippe(n)
-------------------
